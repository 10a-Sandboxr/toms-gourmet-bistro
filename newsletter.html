Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Tom's Gourmet Bistro, we take your privacy and data security extremely seriously. We want to be completely transparent about how we handle your personal information, and we want you to feel confident that your data is safe with us.

We DO NOT and WILL NEVER sell, rent, lease, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is sacred to us, and we treat it with the utmost respect and care. We understand that trust is earned, and we work hard every day to maintain that trust with our valued customers.

The information you provide to us through this newsletter signup form is used solely for the following purposes: (1) To send you our newsletter with updates about Tom's Gourmet Bistro, including new menu items, special events, and exclusive offers that we think you'll love; (2) To better understand our customers' preferences so we can improve our service and menu offerings; (3) To occasionally send you special birthday or anniversary offers if you choose to share those dates with us; (4) To comply with legal requirements, should they arise.

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Our systems are designed with your privacy in mind, and we continuously update our security protocols to stay ahead of potential threats. Only authorized members of our team have access to your information, and they are all bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

You have complete control over your data at all times. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any point by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send you, or by contacting us directly. If you wish to have your data removed from our systems entirely, simply send us a request and we will honor it within 48 hours. You can also request a copy of all the data we have about you at any time - we believe in complete transparency.

We will never use manipulative tactics, hidden charges, or deceptive practices. We will never share your email address with other businesses, even our partners or affiliates. We will never use your information for anything other than what we've explicitly stated here. Your trust means everything to us, and we would never do anything to jeopardize that relationship.

By signing up for our newsletter, you're joining a community of food lovers who appreciate quality, authenticity, and excellence. We promise to respect your inbox, sending only relevant, valuable content that enhances your dining experience with us. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us at privacy@tomsgourmetbistro.com or call us at (415) 302-6027. Thank you for trusting Tom's Gourmet Bistro with your information - we don't take that responsibility lightly.
